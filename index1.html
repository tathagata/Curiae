Electronic Evidence Extraction

Ever wondered why gathering electronic evidence from emails, texts, and social media data is so difficult? We did too, and decided to do something about it. With Curaie, never again will you need to copy and paste, take screenshots, or manually copy records of electronic evidence. All you need are your client’s username and password for collecting email and social network information, and Curaie does the rest. Got a USB port? Just plug in your smartphone and Curaie extracts all the text messages into one easy step.

Easy Sorting and Tagging

Data collection is the easy part. It’s finding the right evidence that can determine the outcome of a case. Timeline

While the devil’s often in the details, any attorney who’s ever built a case knows that the bigger picture can’t be ignored either. Our [clevername] timeline allows you to see both.
