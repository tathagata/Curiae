Simplifying eDiscovey for lawyers

Electronic Evidence Extraction

Ever wondered why gathering electronic evidence from emails, texts, and social media data is so difficult? We did too, and decided to do something about it. With Indico, never again will you need to copy and paste, take screenshots, or manually copy records of electronic evidence. All you need are your client’s username and password for collecting email and social network information, and Indico does the rest. Got a USB port? Just plug in your smartphone and Indico extracts all the text messages into one easy step.

Easy Sorting and Tagging

Data collection is the easy part. It’s finding the right evidence that can determine the outcome of a case. With Indico's easy sorting option, you can view the extracted evidence by sender, date, or message content. Once you’ve found what you need, just star the message and head on over to the timeline to get the bigger picture. Sneek peak of the interface we are building.

Indico Timeline

While the devil’s often in the details, any attorney who’s ever built a case knows that the bigger picture can’t be ignored either. Indico's timeline allows you to see both. All of your previously marked evidence can be seen along a chronological timeline, and you can zoom in or out to see custom-selected time periods in months, weeks, days, hours, or even individual messages.
